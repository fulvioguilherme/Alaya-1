HOW DOES IT WORK?
1. Search

Browse profiles of licensed prenatal massage therapists in your area. Filter by availability, service type & price. Read reviews from other customers. Pick a therapist to suit your budget.

2. Book

Request an appointment with your preferred massage therapist. Receive an email/SMS confirmation and helpful reminders before the therapist comes home.

3. Pay

Your credit card will be charged after your service is complete. No need to hunt for checks or run to the ATM for cash.
