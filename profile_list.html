Kashika Jackson

  • Massage Therapist, DONA Certified Childbirth Doula

Kashika is a Massage Therapist and Dona Certified Birth Doula and as an experienced Doula, Kashika has attended many births including non- medicated natural, medicated natural, VBAC and cesareans births. She believes that labor and delivery should be as wonderful as it can be and Relaxing using Massage Therapy. Her goal for each birth is to help the experience be the best that it can be for the whole family and most especially the mother.
